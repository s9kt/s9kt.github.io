CS @ PSU | Big Silly Goose 2 hours ago

Part 1:
My peers noted the strength of the specific visual design I decided to go with. Unfortunately they didn’t explicitly provide any good feedback I can act upon. However, their interpretation about the audience was incorrect, which means that I can do a better job at trying to target it towards my intended audience of policymakers.
Part 2:
 I used Claude Sonnet by Anthropic instead of MS Copilot because I got a key from the Web Dev club. The responses it gave me were very good feedback. It noted strengths of the visual design but it also gave me actionable feedback I could use to really improve my infographic, such as using more visual properties of iPhones like reactions and the notification dot. It also gave me good tips about how to make text more concise.
Part 3:
 1. I will add a widget bar with some satirical comment to engage the audience better
2. I’ll make my chat text more concise and break it up more to reflect how people actually text each other, as well as use more emojis
3. I will do more research into how to make my format more approachable to my intended audience, or instead make it more reachable to a general audience.
